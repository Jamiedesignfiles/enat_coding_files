Our Story

Enat Kitchen was opened in 2010 by an Ethiopian family wishing to bring authentic Ethiopian cuisine to Portland.

We offer a great variety of entrés featuring dishes made of beef, chicken, fish, or lamb, as well as the vegetarian’s delights such as vegetables, split peas, lentils and salad. We use herbs and spices imported directly from Ethiopia in all of our dishes; providing you with a unique flavor. Cooked to perfection we guarantee that you will have a truly authentic experience.

At Enat, we promise that you will eat to your heart’s content. Dining takes place in a communal setting, where you will eat out of a common plate using your hands; utensils are provided if you wish. Ethiopian food is a fun, tasty, and healthy. So bring your friends and family for a wonderful eating experience coupled with Ethiopian ambience and hospitality.

Of course, an Ethiopian dining experience is never complete without the taste of Ethiopian coffee where it is brewed and served fresh daily. It is a part of Ethiopian tradition that we would like to proudly share with you!

Enat is an Ethiopian term meaning "Mama."
